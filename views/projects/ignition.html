Brief

Project Description: This project explores the journey that a service-based startup goes through starting from the point of inception to expanding into a full-fledged company. Towards the end, based on our system design, analysis of subsystems, the relationships and the gaps between them, we develop a solution which could assist a startup into its growth into an established company.
Project Status: Completed Design Part(Without evaluation)
Client: Course(System's Approach to Design) Project
Guide: Asst. Prof. Pratul Kalita
Project Duration: Sep'15 - Nov'15
Design Responsibilities: Work involved Problem identification, Literature Review, Expert Interviews and Card Sorting, Defning relationships and gaps, Generating Data Flow Diagram, Developing system defnition, Ideation, Conceptualising Final Solution, Wireframing.
